There is no such thing as randomness.

PWDFY is a unique password generator and manager that doesn't work like the traditional ones. The passwords generated with this tool are not random, instead, we use your input data to generate a unique one-way hash that transforms into a secure password.

We use secure, tested and proven methods of hashing input data. We don't store anything and there is no need to remember your password. A unique method that removes two of the most common factors for reusing a password: forgeting and/or losing it.

Want to know more about how we do our magic? Check the Information tab or if you're ready to start being unique, click the button below to generate your awesome password.
